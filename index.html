Data Sources & Methodology

  • Wikipedia: List of fatal snake bites in the United States — individual fatality records
  • CDC/NIOSH: Venomous Snakes at Work — 7,000–8,000 annual estimate
  • Epidemiology of fatal snakebites in the US 1989–2018 — 90.2% rattlesnake share of identified fatalities
  • AAPCC/NPDS Annual Reports — poison center bite counts (2017: 4,071 pit viper; 2021: 3,775 total)
  • FOX 11: Ventura County rattlesnake bites surge 2026 — SoCal 2025/2026 data
  • Irvine Today: Rattlesnake bites spike across SoCal — SoCal 2026 specifics
  • 2026 projections use seasonal weighting from a 2016 study (n=11,138, PMC6887492): Winter 2%, Spring 22%, Summer 50%, Fall 26%. Jan 1 – Apr 17 covers ~12.8% of annual bites (not 29.6% as a linear model assumes), so projections are higher than naive linear extrapolation.
  • Interpolated years (marked ~) use linear interpolation between known NPDS data points.
